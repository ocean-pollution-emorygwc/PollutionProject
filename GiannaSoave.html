Our mission is to fight against Ocean Pollution, but in order to do that, people need to be informed about the issue.

Here are 7 things you probably didn't know about Ocean Pollution

1. over 90% of plastics are not recycled. 2. Plastic is the most common element found in the ocean. 3. Over 1 million seabirds are killed each year because of Ocean Pollution 4. 18 billion pounds of trash winds up in our oceans each year. 5. There is so much trash in some places, people have labeled them dead zones, because it's impossible for marine life to survive there. 6. 80% of sewage that runs into the Mediterranian Sea is untreated, and causes desease. 7. It is estimated that by the year 2050, there will be more trash in the ocean than fish, by weight.
